---

I'm a mechanical engineering student who builds real software. Currently co-founding **GRED**, a service marketplace platform incubated at Kerala Startup Mission — connecting customers with verified local service providers across cleaning, plumbing, electrical, and more. 10% commission model, city-first rollout starting Kakkanad.

My work sits at the overlap of engineering systems, product thinking, and code. I care about building things that actually ship.

Outside the startup: robotics, UAV systems, mechatronics research, and figuring out how AI changes what's possible in physical engineering.

<br/>

---

### What I'm building

**[GRED](https://github.com/ZayanAbdulRahman)** &nbsp;—&nbsp; Service marketplace. Two Flutter apps (Customer + Captain), Firebase backend, real-time order flow. Pre-launch.

**UAV Development** &nbsp;—&nbsp; Autonomous drone work. Pixhawk-based flight control, sensor fusion, mission planning.

**Linear Electromagnetic Shock Absorber** &nbsp;—&nbsp; Research on recovering energy from vehicle vibration through electromagnetic induction. Patent-level concept.

**AI + Mechanical Systems** &nbsp;—&nbsp; Experimenting with demand prediction, IoT + ML on ESP32, and control systems.
